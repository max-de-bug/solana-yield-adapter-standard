# Jupiter LP Adapter

<Info>
**Protocol**: Jupiter Perpetuals  
**Model**: JLP (Jupiter Liquidity Provider) pool  
**Underlying**: USDC  
**Status**: ✅ Live
</Info>

## Overview

The Jupiter adapter integrates with [Jupiter's](https://jup.ag) perpetuals liquidity pool (JLP). Users provide USDC liquidity to the perp market and earn trading fees.

## Protocol-Specific Notes

- JLP pool earns fees from perpetual trading activity
- Uses `add_liquidity` and `remove_liquidity` CPI calls
- Compute budget may need to be increased for complex routing
