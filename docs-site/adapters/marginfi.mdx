## Overview

The MarginFi adapter integrates with [MarginFi's](https://marginfi.com) overcollateralized lending protocol. MarginFi uses a Just-In-Time (JIT) risk engine that evaluates positions in real-time.

## Protocol-Specific Notes

- MarginFi's risk engine operates JIT — no manual "refresh" instruction needed
- `remaining_accounts` must include bank and oracle accounts for risk checks
- Uses `lending_account_deposit` and `lending_account_withdraw` CPI calls
